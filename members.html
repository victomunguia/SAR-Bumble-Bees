Members



Victor Munguia

As a computer engineer and a fanatic of electrical components, Victor Munguia has supported the project by proposing and managing the quality of the components used to make the sensor package and achieve the desired objective of locating victims. In addition, he plays an important role as the chief programmer for the project, creator of interfaces, and embedded programming. In addition, Victor played the the role of main website programmer, designer, and developer.


Aiven Dawood

An electrical engineer with a focus on communication, antenna theory and design, networking, and embedded programming, Aiven Dawood has led the project SAR Bumble Bees with the purpose of helping find victims trapped underneath rubble and debris during the event of an earthquake.



Milan Azet

With an emphasize on power and communication systems, Milan has helped design and illustrate various power circuits and mesh network schemes. His skills span also in the programming field with a focus on embedded programming. He serves also as a graphics creator, modeling and drawing illustrations throughout the project.


Rachelle Ramos

As an electrical engineering student who is enthusiastic about RF and power systems, Rachelle joined the team to demonstrate her capabilities and skills by designing efficient power management circuits and frequency scanning circuits. She serves as the Vice Financial Officer, and a liaison for SAR Bumble Bees.



Eddy Zarate

Aspiring Electrical Engineer Eddy Zarate with expertise on radio frequencies analysis, digital communication and signal processing, has assisted the project through the implementation of instruments capable of detecting wireless communication signals. He serves as an editor and electrical circuit designer.


Francisco Sastre

With a focus on digital signal processing (DSP) and embedded programming, Francisco Sastre has been a contributor for the project since the start. His skills in analog and digital communication makes him a valuable asset to the project. He also serves as the Chief Financial Officer in charge of budget and funding.



Colton Berry

With a keen eye to details, Colton Beery is also an expert in hardware and digital logic designs. His knowledgeable skills in programming allow him to be a flexible contributor to the group through his assistance in the website, and quality monitoring of the components.
