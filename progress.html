Chasis

The task chosen is to focus on implementing a box to fit the sensor package. The objective is to have the sensor packaged into a box that can be flexible, and easily attached to any flying or land drone. Using an enclosing package protects the sensor circuitry from environmental conditions such as humidity, rain, or heat that would cause the components to degrade faster. The design of the box should also enable quick troubleshooting and replacement of internal components.
